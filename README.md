This application allows student teachers to register and keep track of their teaching practice lessons. 📚
Users can record when they teach a class with a supervisor’s observation visit, as well as lessons taught independently without a visit.

The application helps practitioners organise their teaching schedule, monitor completed lessons, and maintain a clear record of their practicum experience throughout the academic year. It also facilitates communication and follow-up between student teachers and supervisors by providing an organised overview of teaching practice activities.
